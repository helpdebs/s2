🎙️ Case audio – Heat exchanger failure
📍 Full transcript (Level 1):
"The thing is, we had a non-conformance in the heat exchanger after just 18 months. What happened was the client changed the operating temperature, but they didn't inform us. At first, I thought it was just a materials issue, but it turned out that the real problem was a combination of factors: higher flow velocity and lower pH. I ended up re-running the whole root cause analysis. Long story short, the corrosion rate increased by 40%, and we had to implement new inspection protocols."
